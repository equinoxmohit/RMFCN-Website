About RMFAN

Real Madrid Fan Association Nepal is a Real Madrid fan association running in Nepal. Real Madrid Fan Association Nepal, shortly known as RMFAN was established in 2016 with the aim of uniting all Real Madrid fan in Nepal. Real Madrid is one of the biggest club in the world and the main objective of this association is to let people know about Real Madrid. RMFAN is not only an association which focuses on Real Madrid, but it is an association which is established to help the standard of the football grow in Nepal.
Further, our members are committed towards development of the country with the help of different events organised by Real Madrid Fan Association Nepal. We, as a citizen of Nepal, are responsible towards the nation and are ready to give our hundred percent for the development of our country Nepal.
Different social events are organised by Real Madrid Fan Association Nepal, which has gained positive response by local people.
Any Real Madrid fan from Nepal are free to join our organisation to help the association grow and expand throughout the country. We plan to expand the association throughout the country and are further committed to open our branches in all the seventy five districts of Nepal.
Further, we are also planning to organise different football tournaments, focusing the young generations, so that Nepali football can develop and grow from the grass-root level. We believe that only after we develop our football from the grassroot level by giving young generation opportunities, the young talents will be able to showcase themselves in front of the world where competition in the field of football is increasing day by day.
We believe that Nepal has not been able to develop in football because of lack of infrastructure. Every member of Real Madrid Fan Association Nepal are committed to develop the infrastructure lacking in football in every way they can. Real Madrid Fan Associtaion Nepal is also planning to organise different charity shows to collect funds from people so as to help the football standard grow by providing the basic infrastructures required in order to perform in a better level.
Let us all be a part of Real Madrid Fan Association and help in the growth of Football in Nepal and also let people know about the greatest club in the world, Real Madrid.

Upcoming Event

Futsal Competition is being held at Maitidevi Futsal at 21/05/2016, which is being organised by Real Madrid Fan Association Nepal.
Anyone who is interested can take part in the event by registering. Registration form are available at the office of RMFAN.
Criterias for getting entry in the competition are:

  • Team should have atleast Seven players.
  • Only teams with no criminal records will be allowed to participate.
  • Registration charge for the competition is Rs.2000.
  • Availability of citizenship or driving license should be their while registering for the competition.

Special Note: After the registration itself, under any circumstances, if any registered team is found violating rules, Real Madrid Fan Association Nepal can withdraw the registration of the team. Further, all members are notified that they are to bring the membership card in order to get free entry in the event.
