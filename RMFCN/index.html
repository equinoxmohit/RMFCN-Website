Upcoming Events

Next Meeting

The next meeting will be held at 17/05/2016 at Kamalpokhari exactly at 10 AM. All active members of Real Madrid Fan Association Nepal are requested to attain the meeting.
The main agendas for the meeting are:

  • Selection of the new President of Real Madrid Fan Association Nepal.
  • Selection of the vice president and other board members of RMFAN.
  • Discussion on how to increase the members of the organisation.
  • Discussion on the social events that are going to be organised.
  • Budget planning for the upcoming year.
  • Discussion on what we can help to improve the standard of Nepali Football.

SPECIAL NOTE: All the members are requested to come up with their membership cards in order to be able to attend the meeting. It is to also notify that anyone who want to be the member of the fan association can register at the event itself.
